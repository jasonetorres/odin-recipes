Lasagna

Lasagna, so good and easy to make. Also SUPER easy to screw up. thankfully with this amazingly easy to follow recipe, yours will end up just like your grams.


First no meal is quality, without quality ingredients.Here is what you'll need:

Ingredients


  • Meat: This super meaty lasagna has sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef.
  • Onion and Garlic: An onion and two cloves of garlic are cooked with the meat to add TONS of flavor.
  • Tomato Products: You'll need a can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of sauce and two cans of paste.
  • Spices: This lasagna recipe is flavored with fresh parsley, basil, salt, italian seasoning, and black pepper.
  • Sugar: Two tablespoons of white sugar add subtle sweetness and flavor to the sauce.
  • Noodles: Use store bought or homemade noodles.
  • Cheese!! Parm, Mozz, Ricotta cheese make this extra decadent.
  • Egg: An egg helps bind the cheese so it doesn't ooze out when you cut into it.

Once you've gathered everything, you can begin.

Steps

  1. Make the sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the filling
  4. Layer the pasta according to recipes/li>
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let Lasagna rest before service
